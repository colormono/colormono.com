## Goals

- **Time-saving:** Facilitate the planning and preparation of meals for the entire week.
- **Promotion of healthy eating:** Offer balanced recipes and portion control.
- **Reduction of food waste:** Optimize the use of ingredients to minimize waste.
- **Customization:** Adapt to the dietary needs and preferences of the family.
- **User-friendly design:** Create an easy-to-use and accessible interface.
