"Fractal" is an exploration of the intersection between dance, theater, and new technologies. The teaser was created in late 2012. We were involved in various aspects of the project, experimenting with a diverse group to combine music, staging, and interactive visuals. Fractal is a preview of a work based on Fran de Lobster's music album "Los colores de la tierra", suggesting the introspective journey of a character.
