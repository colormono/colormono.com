"Locomoci√≥n" it's an experiment who let the user explore the image movement sensation. In this interactive installation the user break a clip into frames, and browse them using the body as a player control. The clips are photography studies from [Eadweard Muybridge](https://www.eadweardmuybridge.co.uk/), this works are the first steps on cinema history.
This is an art+tecnology investigation where the user must to leave the confort of the chair and move to the stage, creating a new dimention in his spectator role.
