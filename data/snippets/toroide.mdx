---

If the player exits on the right side, should back on the left side, and vice versa.
If the player exits on the bottom side, should back on the top side, and vice versa.

```java
if(x > width) x = 0;
if(x < 0) x = width;
if(y > height) y = 0;
if(y < 0) x = height;
```
